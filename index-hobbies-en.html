Beyond my professional and academic pursuits, I have a rich tapestry of personal interests. With a history of playing handball for five years, I have imbibed the values of teamwork and discipline. Handball taught me valuable lessons about teamwork, discipline, and determination, making it a significant part of my life during those years.


My love for the arts is evident in various forms. Music, books and movies bring me joy and inspiration. I find joy in listening to music, exploring diverse genres. Painting is another outlet for my creativity, allowing me to express my thoughts and feelings visually, exploring different techniques and styles. It's a peaceful and fulfilling activity that lets me escape into a world of colors and imagination. Reading books is a constant source of pleasure, offering me the chance to explore new worlds and gain fresh perspectives.


Another hobby that has been a constant companion since my childhood is dancing. In the past, I enjoyed the energetic and lively moves of the shuffle dance, and I also dabbled a bit in folklore dancing. Now, I occasionally engage in Zumba - a form of self-expression and a way to stay active and energized.


When I was a child, I had the opportunity to learn and play the piano. The piano became a cherished part of my early years, offering a creative outlet and a way to connect with the beauty of music.


Besides physical activities and the arts, I've always found joy in the world of computer games. Furthermore, my affinity for computer games, which began in my early years, has persisted, underscoring my appreciation for technology and interactive experiences.
